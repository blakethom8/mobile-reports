Body text uses Arial 14px for readability. Narrative prose uses Georgia (body default). Mix them naturally — Georgia for long-form analysis, Arial for lists, tables, and callouts.

Subsection Heading (h3)

Use h3 for sub-topics within a section. Keep sections focused — one major question or finding per section.
